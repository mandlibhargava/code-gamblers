Our Journey

From seed to canopy—how EcoSphere grew

2018: The Seed is Planted

Spring 2018

Founder Dr. Sarah Green, while conducting field research in the Amazon, envisioned a platform that could connect individual actions to global environmental impact.

2019: First Prototype

Winter 2019

The initial concept—a tree-planting tracker—was developed with a small team of environmental scientists and developers.

2020: Official Launch

Earth Day 2020

EcoSphere launched with 3 core activities and 500 founding members. Our first partnership with OneTreePlanted was established.

2021: Global Expansion

Summer 2021

We expanded to 15 countries and introduced the reporting system, allowing users to document environmental issues in their communities.

2023: 10,000+ Members

Present Day

With over 10,000 active members, 12,500 trees planted, and 3,200 environmental issues reported, EcoSphere continues to grow its impact.

Dr. Sarah Green

Founder & CEO

Dr. Green holds a Ph.D. in Environmental Science from Stanford University. Her research on ecosystem resilience has been published in numerous journals. After witnessing firsthand the devastating effects of deforestation during her fieldwork, she founded EcoSphere to bridge the gap between scientific knowledge and public action.
