Our Mission for a Greener Future

EcoSphere was founded on the belief that collective action can create meaningful environmental change.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a group of environmental scientists and tech enthusiasts, EcoSphere began as a small community project aimed at connecting people with nature through technology.

What started as a simple tree-planting tracker has grown into a global platform with thousands of members working together to reduce pollution, protect ecosystems, and promote sustainable living.

Today, we partner with environmental organizations, schools, and businesses to amplify our impact and create real change.
