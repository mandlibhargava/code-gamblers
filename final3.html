Help protect our planet by documenting pollution, deforestation, and other environmental concerns in your area.

Why Your Reports Matter

Raise Awareness

Bring attention to environmental issues that might otherwise go unnoticed.

Mobilize Action

Your reports help organize community responses and cleanup efforts.

Track Patterns

Documenting issues helps identify patterns and problem areas over time.

How To Report an Issue

1

Document the Issue

Take clear photos or videos of the environmental concern. Include landmarks or location details when safe to do so.

2

Note Important Details

Record the date, time, exact location (GPS coordinates if possible), and any other relevant information about the situation.

3

Submit Your Report

Use our secure form to upload your documentation. Our team will verify and categorize the report.

Safety First

  • Never put yourself in danger to document an issue
  • Avoid trespassing on private property
  • Be aware of your surroundings at all times
  • Don't approach hazardous materials or situations
  • If it's an emergency, contact local authorities first

Air Pollution

Document excessive smoke, industrial emissions, or burning violations.
